Campaign Poster Design

This project involved designing a campaign poster for a student election. The objective was to create a bold, visually striking design that would stand out in a competitive environment.

Project Overview

The focus of this design was clarity, hierarchy, and immediate readability. Campaign materials must communicate quickly, so typography size, contrast, and spacing were carefully balanced to ensure the candidate’s name and message were instantly recognizable from a distance.

Design Focus

  • Strong visual hierarchy
  • High contrast for readability
  • Balanced typography and spacing
  • Clean, modern layout structure

Tools Used

  • Figma
  • Grid-based layout system
  • Color contrast principles
  • Typography emphasis techniques

Outcome

The final design prioritized visibility and impact while maintaining a polished and professional aesthetic. This project strengthened my understanding of persuasive design and visual communication in real-world campaign settings.
